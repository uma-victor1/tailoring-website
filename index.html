who are we

Oversabi stitches is a professional unisex tailoring service. The goal of oversabi stitches is to redefine tailoring in Nigeria by providing easy, efficient, satisfactory and above all timely service at very affordable prices using latest technologies and following latest trends in a very creative environment. Do you have your own design you'd like to make into an outfit? Can't find any design you'd like or do not have any design in mind? Not to worry, we have your back. We offer the cafeteria approach from fabric selection to design selection and outfit Production. We are able to interpret your design and make it into that fabulous outfit you desire. If you don't have any design we are able to make suggestions for you.
